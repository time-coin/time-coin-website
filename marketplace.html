P2P Marketplace

Buy and sell TIME Coin directly with other community members. No intermediary — transactions settle wallet-to-wallet.

How it works

  1. Seller posts amount, price, and their TIME wallet address.
  2. Buyer contacts seller and sends payment (fiat, crypto, or agreed method).
  3. Seller sends TIME Coin to buyer's wallet.
  4. Mark listing as sold when complete.

TIME Coin does not hold funds or mediate disputes. Transact carefully and verify addresses.
