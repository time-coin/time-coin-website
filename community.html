Community Guidelines

Our code of conduct for creating a welcoming, respectful, and productive community around TIME Coin

Introduction

TIME Coin is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

These guidelines exist to ensure our community remains a positive space where members can learn, share, collaborate, and grow together. By participating in the TIME Coin community, you agree to follow these guidelines.

Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in TIME Coin a positive experience for everyone. We expect all community members to show respect and courtesy to each other in all interactions, whether in Discord, Telegram, Reddit, GitHub, or any other TIME Coin space.

Expected Behavior

‚úÖ Do's

  • Be welcoming and friendly: Greet new members and help them get oriented
  • Stay on topic: Keep discussions relevant to TIME Coin and cryptocurrency
  • Use appropriate channels: Post in the correct channel for your topic
  • Search before asking: Check FAQ, docs, and search history before asking questions
  • Share valuable content: Contribute useful information, resources, and insights
  • Give credit: Acknowledge others' contributions and cite sources
  • Help moderate: Report rule violations to moderators
  • Learn from mistakes: Accept feedback gracefully and improve
  • Respect privacy: Don't share personal information about others
  • Use clear communication: Write clearly and proofread before posting

Unacceptable Behavior

üö´ Zero Tolerance Violations

The following behaviors will result in immediate removal from the community:

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on protected characteristics
  • Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual attention
  • Doxxing (sharing private information without consent)
  • Threats of violence or violent language
  • Illegal activity or encouraging illegal activity

Spam & Self-Promotion

NOT ALLOWED:

  • Repeated posting of the same content
  • Unsolicited advertising or promotion
  • Referral link spam
  • Bot spam or automated posting
  • Promoting other cryptocurrencies without permission

Scams & Fraud

NOT ALLOWED:

  • Impersonating team members or moderators
  • Posting fake giveaways or airdrops
  • Phishing attempts or malicious links
  • Fake support DMs asking for private keys/seeds
  • Pump and dump schemes

Disruptive Behavior

NOT ALLOWED:

  • Trolling or intentionally provocative posts
  • Starting or participating in flame wars
  • Excessive negativity or FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt)
  • Going off-topic repeatedly
  • Ignoring moderator warnings

Inappropriate Content

NOT ALLOWED:

  • NSFW content of any kind
  • Graphic violence or gore
  • Excessive profanity or vulgarity
  • Political or religious proselytizing
  • Content that violates intellectual property rights

Channel-Specific Rules

Discord

  • #general: General discussion about TIME Coin
  • #support: Technical support and troubleshooting
  • #development: Developer discussions and code review
  • #trading: Price discussion and market analysis (no financial advice)
  • #masternodes: Masternode setup and operation
  • #announcements: Official announcements only (read-only for members)

Telegram

  • English only in main channel (use local language channels for others)
  • No voice/video messages in main channel
  • Limit GIFs and stickers to avoid spam
  • Use @ mentions sparingly

Reddit

  • Follow Reddit-wide rules and reddiquette
  • Use post flairs appropriately
  • Search before posting duplicate content
  • Upvote quality content, don't downvote just because you disagree

GitHub

  • Follow the contribution guidelines in CONTRIBUTING.md
  • Be professional and constructive in code reviews
  • Use issue templates when reporting bugs
  • Test your code before submitting pull requests

Twitter/X

  • Stay professional in replies to official TIME Coin account (@TIMEcoin)
  • Don't spam mentions or tags
  • Credit sources when sharing information
  • Report impersonation accounts
  • Official account: @TIMEcoin

Moderation

Moderator Responsibilities

Our moderators are community volunteers who help maintain a positive environment. They will:

  • Enforce these guidelines fairly and consistently
  • Warn members who violate guidelines
  • Remove content that violates guidelines
  • Issue temporary or permanent bans when necessary
  • Help answer questions and resolve conflicts

Enforcement Actions

Important Notes

  • Moderators may skip enforcement steps for severe violations
  • Ban evasion (creating new accounts) results in permanent ban
  • Warnings and bans apply across all TIME Coin platforms
  • All moderation actions are logged for transparency

Reporting Violations

How to Report

If you witness or experience behavior that violates these guidelines:

  1. Use platform reporting tools: Discord has built-in reporting, Reddit has report buttons, etc.
  2. Contact moderators directly: Send a DM to any moderator (they have special roles/badges)
  3. Email community team: community@time-coin.io
  4. Include evidence: Screenshots, links, usernames, timestamps

What to Include in Your Report

  • Who: Username(s) of person(s) involved
  • What: Description of the violation
  • When: Date and time
  • Where: Platform and channel
  • Evidence: Screenshots or links

What Happens After You Report

  1. Moderators review your report (usually within 24 hours)
  2. Investigation is conducted if needed
  3. Appropriate action is taken
  4. You receive confirmation (details may be limited for privacy)

Confidentiality

All reports are treated confidentially. Your identity will not be shared with the reported user. We take retaliation seriously and will take action against anyone who retaliates against a reporter.

Appeals Process

If you believe you were banned or warned unfairly, you can appeal:

How to Appeal

  1. Wait at least 24 hours after the action
  2. Email: appeals@time-coin.io
  3. Include:
    • Your username and platform
    • Date of the action
    • Explanation of why you believe the action was unfair
    • Any relevant evidence
  4. Wait for review (typically 3-5 business days)

Appeal Guidelines

  • Be respectful and factual in your appeal
  • One appeal per incident
  • Appeals for zero-tolerance violations are rarely successful
  • Decision on appeal is final

Security & Safety

üîê Protect Yourself from Scams

NEVER:

  • Share your wallet seed phrase with anyone
  • Send TIME or crypto to someone claiming to be from the team
  • Click suspicious links, even from trusted-looking accounts
  • Participate in "giveaways" requiring you to send crypto first
  • Trust DMs from people claiming to be support/moderators

Official Channels Only

TIME Coin team members will ONLY contact you through:

  • Official verified Twitter account
  • Official Discord server
  • Official Telegram channel
  • Email from @time-coin.io domain

We will NEVER DM you first or ask for funds/private keys.

Reporting Scams

If you encounter a scam:

  • Report immediately to moderators
  • Don't engage with the scammer
  • Screenshot evidence
  • Warn others in the community
  • Report to the platform (Discord, Telegram, etc.)
