Community Guidelines

Please read our full community guidelines to understand expected behavior and standards.

Read Full Guidelines
🤝
Be Respectful

Treat all community members with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks will not be tolerated.

💡
Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to TIME Coin, cryptocurrency, and blockchain technology. Use appropriate channels for different topics.

🚫
No Spam or Shilling

Don't spam messages, links, or promote other projects without permission. Share valuable content that helps the community.

🔒
Protect Privacy

Don't share personal information about yourself or others. Be cautious with wallet addresses and transaction details.

⚠️
Beware of Scams

Team members will never DM you first or ask for funds. Report suspicious accounts immediately to moderators.

🎯
Constructive Feedback

We welcome criticism and suggestions. Share feedback constructively to help improve TIME Coin.
